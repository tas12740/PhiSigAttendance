Adam's Tips

  • Bring your stuff for I-Panel and leave it in a friend's car so you don’t have to go back home and then show up to I-Panel late (if they need a car to put it in they can text Sarah Stephens)
  • Bring your own mug to be sustainable if that’s your thing
  • Write out your comments for people in your notes so that when it is time to give your pro/con you can be concise
  • Comfy clothes
  • If you wear contacts, bring your glasses
  • Don't be afraid to speak up on an experience you had with a PNM contrary to someone else, even if the room seems to disagree
  • We're not supposed to come to a consensus, so everyone's opinions are valid! If you had a different experience than someone else, you have just as much right as anyone to voice it! If you're nervous about it, text a mediator.
  • Take notes during the events (especially Friday) so you can read them during I-Panel! Just step out of the room for like 3 minutes (during events) that way you’re not struggling to remember the conversation come Friday
