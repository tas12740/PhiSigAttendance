Requirements & Eligibility

Eligibility

Recruitment is open to 2nd-semester First Year students through 2nd-semester Juniors with an unrounded UNC GPA of 3.0 or higher.

Recruitment is also open to transfer students with at least one semester of UNC-CH coursework with an unrounded 3.0 GPA or higher.

Requirements

During Open Recruitment, you will need to attend three out of the four events, one of which must be the Friday event. You will also need to complete the Open Recruitment Fact Sheet that will be emailed to you (or which can be found under "Recruitment Forms").

If invited back to Closed Recruitment, it is required that you attend three out of the four events, one of which must be the Friday event. Dress for the Friday event is business casual (think "class presentation" dresscode). Again, if you know you will have a scheduling conflict, please notify the Recruitment chairs, Callie and Francisco, before the event.
